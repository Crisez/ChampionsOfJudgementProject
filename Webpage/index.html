Welcome to Champions of Judgement

This project is about building an RTS game for beginners and casual players alike. The idea is to produce a simple but fun game in Unity that will provide a slower paced experience. The pacing will center around viewing the engagements between enemies and learning the basics to RTS games in a simple but fun environment.

The Project

Two gods pit stone champions against each other to determine the fate of the mortal realm in this single player RTS.

General Overview

The game Champions of Judgement takes place in another plane of existence where two gods are holding a contest using medieval styled arenas and stone guardians. Both sides have dedicated troops to their cause that fight to bring down the enemy tower. The god who wins an agreed upon amount of battles will get to decide the fate of the mortals of their shared world.

Additional Information

Champions of Judgement is designed for PC gaming and implements dynamic troop movement through augmented flocking behaviors. Melee based combat in this project leads to large group battles with units entering and breaking formation to effectively fight the enemy. The project also implements an uprade system to improve unit performance throughout the battle.
