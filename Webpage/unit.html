Knight Champion

The Backbone of the Arena armies, these stone knights are bigger and more durable than their followers, the Footmen Champions.

Description

These stone guardians take command of lesser champions, such as the footmen, and lead them into battle. They can be controlled directly and can be told where to position themselves. They in turn give positioning orders to their followers and are able to resupply the frontline with a large assortment of troops.

Stats

Health: 15

Damage: 5

Attack Cooldown: 5

Footmen Champion

Though small, these stone soldiers swarm their enemies when rallied behind a stronger unit.

Description

Footmen Champions are not particularly threatening, but they often travel in a formation with a Knight Champion and serve as additional protection and damage for their stronger leaders. While they deal low amounts of damage, they can strike quicker and are never alone in a fight.

Stats

Health: 10

Damage: 1

Attack Cooldown: 3
